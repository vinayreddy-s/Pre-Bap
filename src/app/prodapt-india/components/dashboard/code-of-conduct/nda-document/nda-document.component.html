3.08 Non-Solicit
3.08.1 Non-solicitation of employee

During the Employee's employment with Prodapt and for a period of two (2) years following the termination of his or her employment with Prodapt, for any reason, Employee agrees to refrain from and will not, directly or indirectly as an independent contractor, employee, consultant, agent, partner, joint venture or otherwise solicit or take away, or attempt to solicit or take away employees of Prodapt, either for Employee's own business or for any other person or entity.

3.08.2 Non-solicitation of clients etc.

Employee agrees that during the time the Employee is employed by Prodapt and for a period of twenty four (24) months after termination of Employee's employment with Prodapt, for any reason, Employee shall not, directly or indirectly, either for the Employee or on behalf of any other person, firm, company or corporation:

(i) Solicit, divert, or take away any of the existing customers of Prodapt.
(ii) Solicit or accept business from or provide services to or accept employment with or seek remuneration from any of the clients or customers of Prodapt or any entity under common control of any client or customer of Prodapt. For purposes of this Agreement, a client or customer of Prodapt shall be defined as any person, firm, partnership, corporation or other entity with or to whom Prodapt (i) sold goods or services, (ii) had submitted a written proposal for a specific requirement, or (iii) had work in progress, as of the date of Employee's termination of employment or during the twenty four (24) months prior to the termination of employment of Employee; or
(iii) Render services as an employee, consultant or otherwise, to or assist with the creation of any Competing Organization or associate or join direct competitors.

NOTE: For the purposes of this clause, a "Competing Organization" means any person or organization, including Employee which (i) is engaged in, or about to become engaged in, the design, development, integration, implementation, sale, marketing or distribution of software applications which are identical or substantially similar to any product provided by Prodapt in existence or under development during the time of Employee's employment with Prodapt or (ii) is engaged in, or about to become engaged, in the provision of software consulting or design/development services which are identical to or substantially similar to the services provided by or procedures and methodologies used by or planned to be used by Prodapt during the Employee's employment with Prodapt.
